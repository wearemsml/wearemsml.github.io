WHO WE ARE

Youth Prowess Zambia Limited (YPZ) is a non-profit making organization that was registered in October 2021. The organization was created by the young people who have the utmost desire of helping their fellow young people by creating a platform where they can engage and share information about issues that affect them. Further, the organization seeks to ensure increased participation of the young people social and economic development process of Zambia.

OUR MISSION

​Youth Prowess Zambia aims at providing a dais on which the youth can discuss matters that contribute to the socioeconomic development of the country and be the voice of the voiceless. For this purpose, the mission statement of YPZ is: Igniting youth’s potential for a better Zambia.

VALUES WE HOLD

​ Founded in 2021, Youth Prowess Zambia has become an integral part of the nation. At Youth Prowess Zambia, we provide unique, life-changing experiences to all of the community’s young minds. We believe in and practice the true act of caring and know how important it is to demonstrate a sincere concern for others. We hold compassion, generosity, and kindness close to our hearts, and go the extra mile to provide our youth with a wide range of opportunities that will allow them to reach their potential.
