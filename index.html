A LITTLE ABOUT US

Youth Prowess Zambia Limited (YPZ) is non-profit making organization that was registered in October 2021. The organization was created by the young people who have the utmost desire of helping their fellow young people by creating a platform where they can engage and share information about issues that affect them. Further, the organization seeks to ensure increased participation of the young people social and economic development process of Zambia.
